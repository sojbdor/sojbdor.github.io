If it doesn't work, refresh your page, see if you have the right version of your web browser from Firefox version 4, Google Chrome version 9 or Safari version 5.1 until the most recent. Test Chrome. Enter "about:flags" in the address bar. Search for Webgl and then activate the option. Firefox. Enter "about:config" in the address bar. Search for "webgl.force-enabled" and change the value to "true". If nothing happens, it's probably due to your graphic card.
