Hey it's me. I created this envolving game for my portfolio to show you my creations of drawings, paintings and 3D. Load the game can take up to 30min or more, perfect for tea time. I am working on another version which will be faster to load the game, to be sure that it can be playing for everyone. If it doesn't work look if you have Firefox version 4, Google Chrome version 9 or Safari version 5.1. If you don't have the time or a good computer, refresh your page or leave.

Press the spacebar to jump, left right for the direction, top and bottom for advance and back off
