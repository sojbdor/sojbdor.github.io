Connecting the Energy or L'ère du Porn, 2019, installation of canvas, galerie du quai, Toulouse. Photo: Marion Lefeuvre
Vue of an exhibition "Connecting Energy" or "Lere du Porn" thank you for your help: Julia Castel, Éléonore Verger, Marion Lefeuvre, Naomie Henry, Alyzée Tricat, Romane Laillet, 2019
Installation of canvas, aluminium and x-plastics with Marion Lefeuvre, 3 computers, 2 projecteurs, aluminium, plastics, food plastics, on canvas. Drawings, pastels, felts. Paintings, acrylic on canvas. Photography, silver. Paitings on cardboard with Romane Laillet
After watching the documentary made on HPG, "There is no sexual intercourse" by Raphael Siboni, I created a space with the color of the science fiction, to create a post-internet space in a chaotic and dystopian atmosphere.
