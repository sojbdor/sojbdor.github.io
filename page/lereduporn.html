After watching the documentary made on HPG, "There is no sexual intercourse" by Raphael Siboni, I became interested in the word "pornography" and all its informal aspect. I found this film perfectly suited to our current existence, immersed in the midst of social networks. That's why I created a space with the color of the science fiction in a confusing way. In oder to create a post-internet space with a chaotic and dystopian atmosphere, like a movie front, to play with the word "pornography" and making it seem like the internet is dead. How to resume a life without the internet?
