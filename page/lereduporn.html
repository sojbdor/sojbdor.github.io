After watching the documentary made on HPG, There is no sexual intercourse by Raphael Siboni, she became interested in the word pornography and what it mean nowadays, with all its history and its informal aspect, that film was perfectly suited to our current existence immersed in the midst of social networks, with our social media addictions so I created a space with the color of the science fiction in a confusing way. In oder to create a post-internet space with a chaotic and dystopian atmosphere, like a movie front, to turn around the word pornography and making it seem like the internet is dead. How to resume a life without the internet?
