ABSTRS - Invasive (Sukkube Remix)[FAM076]

Sukkube released a remix for ABSTRS Gothenburg based ABSTRS is born in San Diego, California and raised in Stockholm, Sweden. His style can be described as raw fast paced techno, even psychedelic and hypnotic at times, almost like a soundtrack to the industrial areas of his hometown. ABSTRS is somewhat of a local hero in the underground scene of Gothenburg, co-founding the Tekla collective, plus hosting numerous raves and parties in abandoned buildings, in and around the city with his Obalans and Sequence projects

Written by ABSTRS
Master: Vladimir Mihajlovic
Label: Fallen Metropolis
Artwork: Christian Rutz
Released May 23, 2023

Kagami Smile - A Void (Sukkube x Сељак Remix)[FAM088]

Sukkube feat. Сељак released a remix for Kagami Smile, is an artist from an industrial city a few hours from Chicago. He takes influence from shoegaze records as well as minimal techno labels such as Chain Reaction and Modern Love. His most recent output of music focuses on minimal meditative synthworks and strong nods to Japanese video game soundtracks as well as the concept of liminal spaces. His music reflects a series of recurring dreams had since 2017, that of a missed moment with a stranger
