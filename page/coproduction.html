Lost Sonar Collective, documentary film live act, 2023

Lostsonar Collective Production
Producers: Hara Koukouvinou, Bernhard Range
Camera: Kimbow, Bernhard Range, Charlene Levasseur
Post-production: Hartbrand
Mastering: Hartbrand
Set Photographer: Kenneth Scott

Born out of one of the darkest moments the global techno scene has faced in it's history, the lost sonar collective offers a much needed platform for live techno artists of Berlin and its surroundings to express their intricate artistry. Using various lost places and lost dance floors to capture high quality immersive live performances set against striking brutalist scenery.
