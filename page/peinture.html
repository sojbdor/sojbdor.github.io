For every new painting, Charlène Levasseur do some researches. She draws her inspiration from contemporary art books, modern art books, Russian modern art books, fashion photographs, concert photographs and album artworks. Sometimes she inspires herself with her own photographs and drawings, mixed with dinosaurs and animals old books. In her paintings, she built the form with black color as a labyrinth, as a sort of unknown place with sectorized places. The black shape comes to create a kind of cartography, like the synapses of neurons, places are all related each other throughout borders. After, she puts colors. She begins to put with the most dark color then she clear the color with white, which then gives a pure color, raw, solid, opaque and clear. This process creates a discontinuous gradient, like a sort of rainbow of liberty. Sometimes it representes many inner spaces, occupied by sexy cyber woman punks who are in love, smoking cigarets, swimming, playing music, listening to music, dancing, drinking, sometimes together, occasionnally alone.
