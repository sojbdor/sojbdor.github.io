Geographic Melody








Geographic Melody, 2019, pencil on paper, 29,7 x 21 cm


Serie of 7 drawings, who reflects the language of the body and music emerge into a single object, a drawing. This serie was made, spontaneously, without any break while listening to techno music at the same time. Each drawing represents a very distinct music. It can translate the emotions that music evokes into lignes and doodles.







Psych Atelier, 2019, with @j_u_l_i_a_z_e and Mia More, 13 marble papers, 29,7 x 42 cm, Toulouse, France

During a workshop with by Julia Castel, Mia More, Anne Lise Verdier. Paper marbling is a method of aqueous surface design, which can produce patterns similar to smooth marble or other kinds of stone. The patterns are the result of color floated on either plain water or a viscous solution known as size, and then carefully transfer- red to an absorbent surface, such as paper or fabric
