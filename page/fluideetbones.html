Fluides And Bones

At the moment, she is interested in contemporary art in the USSR and Russia (1950-2000). She appreciates the group of artists AES+F, then particularly Vladimir Zakharon, Andrei Filippov, Larissa Zvezdotchetovo-Rezun, Boris Touretski and many others. In her work, painting, video or sound, the recurring theme is the language of the music. She devotes all her time to painting alternating with making music and producing video. She feels connected with the human and nature, the subjects
