No one really knows what planet Sojbdor was born from. Sojbdor doesn't talk much. Sojbdor prefers raspberries. Sojbdor likes raspberries, Sojbdor eats raspberries. Sojbdor likes to listen to Hard-techno, Ambient-techno, Modern-techno, House, Groovy, Experimental things and especially new Techno

UNICREAM, video game, 2019

Is a sort of paradise portfolio in a immersive 3D space, in it first version UNICREAM is online from the end of 2019. In UNICREAM, the player in real time is free to watched the Sojbdor's archive, with paintings, drawings, installations. As a free museum online, you are free to explore this new world and meet eccentric people who got some bugs, they can't move, they don't talk... The work provokes questions : What is real? Where are you? How can we see virtual art today ?
