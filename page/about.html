Charlene Levasseur
Multimedia artist
sojbdor@gmail.com

HTML5, CSS, Java, Json, Unity, C4D, Unity, Ai, Id, Pr, Br, Ps, Ae, Fl, Traktor, Ableton Live

Charlène Levasseur's paintings depict scenes from digital provenance and post technological landscapes. Composed of organic shapes, colored sensual motifs and dripping texts. Born and raised in the South West of France. She's studying at the University of Art of Bordeaux and at the institute superior of art of Toulouse (isdaT), France. She learned painting, video and sound.

At the end of 2019, as an artist who is excited about visuals and sound alike her recent films are created in a pursuit of a synesthetic relationship between image and sound. Her last abstract film is Don’t Walk Alone in collaboration with the artist Pablo Mateo from the Label Figure and she created her first public video game called UNICREAM.

Aside from Charlene’s personal work she has collaborated with other artists, labels and organisations such as, Insel Galerie, Armen Crew, Figure, Midnight Fantasy Entertainment, CampusFM94, radioUDON, Les Siestes Electroniques, Acolora, Roven, FIFIB, La Nuit du Cinéma, Festival Olympe de Gouges, La Forêt Electrique, isdaT, Ensad, Michel Montaigne Bordeaux III, Augustin Museum, Jose Cabanis Media Library, la Cartoucherie, le Printemps de Septembre, l’Annexe, le DadA, le Synopsis, Kit: Exhibition Space, Aparte, Le Cri de la Mouette and Forbiden Zone.
