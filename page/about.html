Biography

Sukkube was smoothly born in the Toulouse alternative scene, from local parties to renowned stages. Constantly creating a bunch of quality productions, label head of Associated Paraboloid, the French producer now works with Diffuse Reality.

Her field of study is adventurous, unrefined, ambient on cinematic techno beats, ugly drums on warm hypnotic melodies, to introduce ghostly vocals and a remarkable environmental breathing layer. Sukkube’s addiction for analog modules and drum machines takes her to build on dry modular live experiments.

French artist for more than a decade, remaining present by expressing her thoughts in the music live scene in Berlin. By working with artist like the Russian boss Roma Zuckerman, labels such as Figure, Fallen Metropolis, Diffuse Reality or recently for Techno Mühely or Lostsonar Collective, she always stayed active and re-invented herself while painting her releases with a clearly own palette that is full of colors and timeless sound-design.

Aside from Sukkube’s personal work she works with: Lost N’ Found, Lostsonar Collective, Exiles Electronics,Techno Mühely, Diffuse Reality, Fallen Metropolis, Associated Paraboloid, Brvtalist, Officina Neukölln, Berlin Modular Society, Tapetown, Codec992, Le Caméléon, Collective Russia, KRASAVA35, Ved Siden Af, gART.n, Sphere Radio, Rhythm Cult, Gaswerksiedlung, Galleri Heike Arndt DK, Insel Galerie, Midnight Fantasy Entertainment, CampusFM, radioUDON, Armen Crew, L’Annexe, Les Siestes Electroniques, Roven, isdaT, Ensad, Michel Montaigne Bordeaux III, FIFIB, La Forêt Electrique, Jose Cabanis Media Library, La Cartoucherie, Le Printemps de Septembre, The Night Of The Cinema Of Gouges, Festival Olympe de Gouges, le DadA, le Synopsis, Kit: Exhibition Space, Aparte, Le Cri de la Mouette and Forbidden Zone.
