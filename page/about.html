Charlène Levasseur is an visual artist and techno producer residing in Berlin. Born and raised in the South West of France. She graduated from the institute superior of Art of Toulouse in 2018, where she studied the painting media program and sound. While in isdaT, she discovered animation through the Adobe After Effects and Avid Media Composer. She quickly became addicted to the art of moving images. She has been involved in the creation of animated works since 2015. Her enthusiasm led her, with 9 artists from around France to the foundation of a label of art and music called Acolora, devoted to contemporary art.

In 2019, Charlène Levasseur relocated to Berlin to take part in the art scene of Germany. As an artist who is excited about visuals and sound alike her recent films are created in a pursuit of a synesthetic relationship between image and sound. Her last abstract film is Don’t Walk Alone in collaboration with a sound artist Pablo Mateo from the Label Figure. In the end of 2019 she created her first public video game called UNICREAM.

Aside from Charlene’s personal work she has collaborated with other artists, labels and organisations such as, Armen Crew, Figure, Midnight Fantasy Entertainment, CampusFM94, radioUDON, Les Siestes Electroniques, Acolora, Roven, FIFIB, La Nuit du Cinéma, Festival Olympe de Gouges, La Forêt Electrique, isdaT, Ensad, Michel Montaigne Bordeaux III, Augustin Museum, Jose Cabanis Media Library, la Cartoucherie, le Printemps de Septembre, l’Annexe, le DadA, le Synopsis, Kit: Exhibition Space, Aparte, Le Cri de la Mouette and Forbiden Zone.
