        CHARLENE LEVASSEUR

        music producer, filmmaker, painter
        contact@charlenelevasseur.com

        HTML5, CSS, Java, Json, Unity, C4D, Unity, Lightroom, Ai, Id, Pr, Br, Ps, Ae, Fl, Traktor, Ableton Live, Pro Tools

        A human being born and raised in the South West of France, Charlene studied the theory of contemporary art at the University of Art Michel Montaigne Bordeaux, after painting and new media at the institute superior of art of Toulouse, isdaT

        Charlene proposes a visual reading of techno music. In 2019, the artist produces a short film called "Don't Walk Alone" for the Dj and producer Pablo Mateo from the label Figure. In 2020, she releases a short film called "Summer's Cell" for the producer and Dj Halosaur from the label Rhythm Cult. Now a mysterious project called "Maracuja" for the label R&S records is up comming.

        Mostly a producer and music lover, Charlene explores the "what is now" with a broader idea, the present. In 2017, she founded the label and collective ACOLORA. Since 2020, she has created the radio of the Gaswerksiedlung, in Berlin. She is mainly occupied by making techno podcasts of one hours per month of studio mixing based on new releases, unreleases of techno, from own productions, to friend's productions, guests, people that she likes, Sans Blabla at CampusFM Radio in Toulouse and KRKRKR at Sphere Radio in Leipzig. Don't hesitate to send her your music.

        When she finds a time free from all negative thoughts about our consumerist and neo liberal universe, Charlene starts to paint. She depicts scenes from digital heritage and cerebral landscapes, composed of organic forms, coloured sensual patterns with black outline mixed sometimes with dripping texts. She likes the combinaison of alternative projects oscillating between painting, video and sound. She creates atmospheric installations where any mediums can find a place together.

        Aside from Charlene’s personal work she has collaborated with Djs, producers, labels and organisations, Blvsh, Sphere Radio, Rhythm Cult, Gaswerksiedlung, Galleri Heike Arndt DK, Insel Galerie, Armen Crew, Figure, Midnight Fantasy Entertainment, CampusFM, radioUDON, L’Annexe, Les Siestes Electroniques, Acolora, Roven, isdaT, Ensad, Michel Montaigne Bordeaux III, FIFIB, La Forêt Electrique, Jose Cabanis Media Library, La Cartoucherie, Le Printemps de Septembre, The Night Of The Cinema Of Gouges, Festival Olympe de Gouges, le DadA, le Synopsis, Kit: Exhibition Space, Aparte, Le Cri de la Mouette and Forbiden Zone
