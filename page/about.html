Charlene Levasseur
Audio Visuel Artist
sojbdor@gmail.com

HTML5, CSS, Java, Json, Unity, C4D, Unity, Ai, Id, Pr, Br, Ps, Ae, Fl, Traktor, Ableton Live, Pro Tools

A human being born and raised in the South West of France, she studied painting and new media at the institute superior of art of Toulouse, isdaT and the theory of contemporary art at the University of Art Michel Montaigne Bordeaux. Mostly a music lover, Charlene explores a post-industrial universe. She proposes a visual reading of techno music. She proposes intuitive and alternative projects oscillating between painting, video and sound. She likes to create atmospheric installations where any medium can find a place together.

She depicts scenes from digital heritage and post-technological landscapes, composed of organic forms, coloured sensual patterns with black outline mixed sometimes with dripping texts

Aside from Charlene’s personal work she has collaborated with Djs, producers, labels and organisations, Rhythm Cult, Gaswerksiedlung, Galleri Heike Arndt DK, Insel Galerie, Armen Crew, Figure, Midnight Fantasy Entertainment, CampusFM, radioUDON, L’Annexe, Les Siestes Electroniques, Acolora, Roven, isdaT, Ensad, Michel Montaigne Bordeaux III, FIFIB, La Forêt Electrique, Jose Cabanis Media Library, La Cartoucherie, Le Printemps de Septembre, The Night Of The Cinema Of Gouges, Festival Olympe de Gouges, le DadA, le Synopsis, Kit: Exhibition Space, Aparte, Le Cri de la Mouette and Forbiden Zone
