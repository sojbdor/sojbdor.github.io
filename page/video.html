Photography film, restitution of workshop with Patricia Morosan, 2024





Premiere coming in 2025. An independent photography film featuring interviews images taken in a intensive workshop during summer 2024, including images from Anna Sweden, Marie Julliard, Vincent Boissonnat, Catherine, Virginie Gueguen. The photography film is based around the idea of taking pictures of Douelle a peacefull village located 20 minutes by car from Cahors, and surrounded by vineyards. The village, with its charismatic inhabitants, the Antinea along the Lot River, and the Causses trails, offers a wide range of activities and endless opportunities for exploration.

Director by Julia Castel and Patricia Morosan
Edited by Julia Castel and Patricia Morosan
Sound design : Charlène Levasseur



Lost Sonar Collective, documentary film live act, 2023



Born out of one of the darkest moments the global techno scene has faced in it's history, the lost sonar collective offers a much needed platform for live techno artists of Berlin and its surroundings to express their intricate artistry.
