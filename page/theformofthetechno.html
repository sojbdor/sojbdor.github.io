The Form Of The Techno

After a few years dealing with the institutional conventions of contemporary art rules and their contradictions, Charlene felt the need to break away from them in order to create a new language that expresses itself freely. The Form of The Techno is a project that casts light on techno music. It depicts scenes from digital heritage, cerebral landscapes, composed of organic forms, sensual colored patterns with black and dripping contours. Resembling infections of plants and 3D sculptures, atoms or anatomical limbs, this project expands on current trends in contemporary art and on the obligations, orders and digital models to be followed to be accepted in the game. It is massive, aggressive with brilliant contrasts, with techno music as its only direction and the importance that its auditory aspect can have on the repercussions of the painted gestures. It is a kind of auditory and real experimentation that adapts its design, colors and movements to the modifications induced by sounds.
