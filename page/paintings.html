Non architect / A-current form



Non architect / A-current form is massive, abstract and colorful. Written with techno in mind, it shows the importance of the auditory experience because of its repercussions on the painted gestures. It is a kind of real-time auditory experimentation by which design, colors and movements adapt to and are changed by sounds.

It depicts scenes from digital heritage in a metaphoric way, mixing landscapes, organic forms, dirty textures, sensual colored patterns. Resembling body infections, 3D sculptures, atoms or anatomical limbs.
