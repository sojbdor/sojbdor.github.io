Charlène / Sukkube is a performer and producer on modular synthesisers. She writes and produces soundtracks for film, theatre, galleries, clubs and labels. In 2019, she graduated from the Institut Supérieur des Arts de Toulouse (ISDAT). Two years later, in 2021, she performed on the Eurorack synthesiser and presented her first album, World In Data, at the legendary Ved Siden Af club in Copenhagen. That same year, she collaborated with group Les Éphémères for a performance at the g.ART.n club in Berlin. In 2022, Charlène presents Red Carpet Incubator in a performance at the Ava club in Berlin. In 2023, she took part in the festival organised by Force Inc. Music Works and Mille Plateaux, with the Non Ference exhibition at the Forum Stadtpark museum in Graz. In 2024, she joined the IPN collective, where she continues to experiment with sound and pushing the boundaries of her musical creations. In May of the same year, she improvised a set at the Seebühne the Seebühne stage at the international Superbooth festival in Berlin, alongside Jessica Kert
