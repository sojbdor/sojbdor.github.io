Sukkube is Charlène Levasseur's musical project. She explores sound synthesis and musique concrète through a modular synthesiser, mixing sequences and samples. Her favourite themes, war and love, guide her artistic work: her writing reflects an exploration of the tensions between conflict and frenzy and offers a space for reflection on internal struggles and complex emotions. Sukkube offers a bittersweet musical experience, where each note and sample tells a story of resilience and vulnerability.

Graduating from the Institut Supérieur des Arts de Toulouse in 2019, she began performing live in 2021. She has played at iconic electronic music venues in Germany, Denmark and Austria

In 2024, she joined the IPN collective, where she continues her research, produces events and teaches electronic music. Her productions have been released on labels such as Detroit Underground, Augmented Research, Associate Records, Diffuse Reality and être audio.
