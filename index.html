Opening of Andrea Taeggi, Jessica Kert, Halosaur, Abstrs

• Sukkube makes melodic and unique techno music. Her style is inspired by artists such as Portishead, Telepopmusik and Lady Startlight. She works alone and uses a modular synthesiser.

After graduating from the Institut Superior of Arts in Toulouse in 2019, she have been performing in public since 2021. She has had the opportunity to play at iconic venues on the electronic music scene

Selection of venues and festivals:
Festival Superbooth (Berlin), AVA Club (Berlin), Acud Macht Neue (Berlin), gART.n (Berlin), Ved Siden Af (Copenhagen), Forum Club (Graz), Le Groove (Geneva)

Her tracks has been released on labels such as:
Detroit Underground, Augmented Research, Associate Records, BCCO, The Brvtalist, HATE, Hate Lab, Techno Muhely, Fallen Metropolis, and Diffuse Reality.

• She joined the Collectif IPN, where she mix and master her own productions as well as external commissions (film, short film, album / EP / VA of musicians). She also co-organize concerts at IPN's showroom (Unplugged Music For Fools, Tramhaus, Parnsnip, Rod-r, CCTemple, Deuce, Even the ghost, Charette Fantôme, Aya Ando, Wolken, Baraque à free, Ourse & Brute, Combinaison Voltaïque).
