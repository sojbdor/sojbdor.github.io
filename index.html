I am Charlène Levasseur from the Sukkube project. I create electronic music that is both mysterious and captivating using a modular synthesiser. My world is inspired by artists such as Portishead, Telepopmusik and Lady Starlight. I work alone and use a modular synthesiser. After graduating from the Institut Supérieur des Arts in Toulouse in 2019, I have been performing in public since 2021. I have had the opportunity to play at iconic venues on the electronic music scene

I joined the IPN collective, where I mix and master my own productions as well as external commissions (short films, feature films, musicians). I also co-produce events. My tracks have been released on labels such as Detroit Underground, Force Inc. / mille plateaux, Augmented Research, BCCO, The Brvtalist, HATE, Hate Lab, Techno Muhely, Associate Records, Fallen Metropolis, and Diffuse Reality.
