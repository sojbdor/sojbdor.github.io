• Sukkube (1994) is a French DJ, musician, and electronic music producer. She creates melodic,
singular techno using modular synthesizers. Her style draws inspiration from artists such as
Portishead, Télépopmusik, and Lady Starlight.


• After graduating from isdaT (institut Superior of Arts of Toulouse) in 2019, she moved to Berlin,
where she has been performing publicly since 2021. Known for her immersive live sonic performances,
she has played at iconic venues and festivals on the electronic music scene, including
Superbooth (Berlin), AVA Club (Berlin), Acud Macht Neu (Berlin), gART.n (Berlin),
Ved Siden Af (Copenhagen), Forum Club (Graz) and Le Groove (Geneva).


• In 2024, she joined the Collectif IPN in Toulouse, where she continues to experiment
with sound and push the boundaries of her musical practice. Founder of the label Associate Records,
she mixes and masters her own productions as well as external commissions (soundtracks for
cinema and theater, albums/EPs for other musicians). She also co-organizes concerts at IPN’s
exhibition space (Tramhaus, Parsnip, Rod-r, CCTemple, Deuce, Even the Ghost, Charette Fantôme,
Aya Ando, Wolken, Ourse & Brute, Combinaison Voltaïque, Ayant, Hugo de l’eau,
Miss Stress, collectif 2035, Chevalière du Jazz, KAAARST, Baraque à Free).


• Her tracks have been released on labels such as Detroit Underground, Augmented Research, BCCO,
Associte Records, Hate, Hate Lab, Diffuse Reality, Brvtalist, examine-archive, Brvtalist, Fallen Metropolis, Techno Muhely.
