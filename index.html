Sukkube is a musician, dj, and music producer based in France. She creates melodic, singular techno using modular synthesizers. Her style draws inspiration from artists such as Ben Klock, Burial and Andy Stott.

At 6 years old, her grandmother gave her a Yamaha synthesizer. Her musical journey began at that moment; her mother was listening to Depeche Mode. She learned guitar with her neighbor and formed her first band, an emo rock group. Later, she met Wizard (aka Clémentine Méreau), who showed her some tricks on Ableton.

In 2016, she co-founded a label called Acolora, focusing on contemporary music of all genres. That same year, she discovered Dommune radio and stumbled upon DJ sets by Bruna, Stanislav Tolkachev, and tracks by Porter Ricks, Positive Centre, DJ Rush, Roma Zuckerman, and Peter Van Hoesen.

After graduating from isdaT (institut Superior of Arts of Toulouse) in 2019, she moved to Berlin, where she has been performing publicly since 2021. Known for her immersive live sonic performances,
she has played at iconic venues and festivals on the electronic music scene, including:
About Blank (Berlin), Superbooth (Berlin), Ved Siden Af (Copenhagen), Forum Klub (Graz), Le Groove (Geneva), Metronum (Toulouse), Le Printemps de Bourges.

In 2021, she founded Associate Records, a label of deviant techno. She curates various artists and produces editions of tapes with raw experimental techno artists.

Since 2023, she has been working with Force Inc. Mille Plateaux as a modular live act performer. Her tracks have been released on labels such as Detroit Underground, Augmented Research, BCCO, Associte Records, Hate, Hate Lab, Diffuse Reality, Brvtalist, examine-archive, Fallen Metropolis, Techno Muhely.
